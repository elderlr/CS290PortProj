Alphabetical Attributes- An Introduction

Through the most carless of observations and via the most un-scientific approach possible; we have established a letter personality matrix. This letter personality matrix can guide you in life’s smallest choices.

Questions such as:

  1. What to name my child?
  2. Who should I pick as a life partner?
  3. Where on the Planet should I live?

Move forward in your Alphabet Association journey and go to the Alphabet Associate page to learn more about the different letter groups.
